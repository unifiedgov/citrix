Before phoning the Service Desk about your password (Google or Modern Desktop) we would like to make you aware that the Council’s password policy has changed.
 
There are two significant changes: Firstly, and in line with the Government's latest advice on passwords, we have made it easier for you!  You will now need to create a 12 character password but this will not expire. However, If you suspect your password has been compromised at any time, you must change it immediately.

Your Modern Desktop password (when you next change it) MUST be at least 12 characters

It will need to include characters from at least three of the four following categories:

  • uppercase letters

  • lowercase letters

  • numbers

  • symbols/punctuation, for example: ~!@#$%^&*_-+=`|\(){}[]:;”‘<>,.?/

Secondly:

Your Google password is going to be the same as your Modern Desktop password

When you change your Modern Desktop password it will also then synchronise with Google to be your new Google password.   


And please remember these important steps…..
 
Keep your passwords safe by not writing them down.
Never share your passwords.
Never re-use passwords between work and home
